Description

Easy Pose is a basic seated posture that helps improve posture and promotes calmness.

Method

Sit on the floor with crossed legs, keeping the spine erect, and place your hands gently on your knees.

Anatomical Analysis

This pose helps align the vertebrae and releases tension in the back muscles.

Description

Lotus Pose is a meditative posture that calms the mind and enhances concentration.

Method

Gently place each foot on the opposite thigh, ensuring the knees face outward.

Anatomical Analysis

This pose opens up the groin and hip muscles while encouraging proper alignment of the pelvis.

Description

Hero Pose is a seated posture that stretches the legs and improves digestion.

Method

Sit with your legs together and folded to one side, keeping your back straight.

Anatomical Analysis

This pose promotes flexibility in the hip joints and strengthens the leg muscles.
